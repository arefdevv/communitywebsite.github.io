About Us

Samen Al Hojaj Social & Cultural Community was established in 2018.
Most members of this community is made up of Youngers, Teenagers and young adults who'm are in school, high school and
university completing their studies in a range of different fields to become a beneficial figure for the society here in Australia.
Every year the managers of Samen Al Hojaj host a range of events and festival such as Nawroz Festival,
Eid Qurban Festival also known as Eid al-Adha Festival and also Muharram which is a very important month for all the Shia Muslims around the world. Samen Al Hojaj also host a yearly event for students who finished their year 12 VCE studies and are starting
university to congratulate them on reaching the milestone and moving on to the next.
